Our Team

Wout Albers

Attorney in Amsterdam, the Netherlands
Wout started his career as a litigation lawyer in 2012, putting into practice his enormous professional and personal involvement towards social, environmental, and human rights related legal issues. Throughout the years, thanks to his heart for the protection of the vulnerable and due to his participation in several human rights related “fact-finding-missions” in Latin America, he developped a high level of knowledge and expertise in the field of international human rights and environmental law.

Wout speaks Dutch, English, German, French and Spanish and has been a member of the Latin America focus group of Dutch non-profit organization Lawyers for Lawyers for more than seven years. He is also one of the founders and board members of the Dutch section of the UK based organization Caravana de Juristas a Colombia.

  • Environmental law

Adil José Meléndez Marquez

Attorney in Cartagena de Indias, Colombia
Adil is an experienced litigation lawyer specialized in the protection of human rights, transitional justice, administration law, environmental law, and labor law. He has been representing victims of human rights violations in Colombian conflict areas since 2006. Moreover, Adil is dedicated to the protection of nature, biodiversity and living environment of indigenous people threatened by the presence and activities of national and multinational corporations. His extensive experience, focus on teamwork, and respect for the most vulnerable people of the world, are the fundamental pilars of Adil’s mission in life.

Adil is a member of the Caravana Internacional de Juristas and the Alianza Mundial de Mandatorios y Líderes Africanos y Afrodescendientes, both NGO’s founded for the protection of human rights.

Field of expertise
  • Human Rights
  • Transitional justice
  • Environmental law
  • Administration law
  • Labor law

Ron Rosenhart Rodriguez

Legal advisor
Ron specializes in Dutch and international corporate law and corporate governance. By connecting his professional expertise to a strong personal interest for the protection of human rights in Latin America, he advises on legal issues concerning victims of environmental and human rights violations committed by local and multinational companies across the globe.

Ron holds a master’s degree in Dutch private law (graduated in 2013), is fluent in Dutch, English and Spanish, and is a member of the Latin America focus group of Dutch non-profit organization Lawyers for Lawyers.
