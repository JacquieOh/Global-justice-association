"We hold multinationals accountable"

We represent victims in and out of court to seek justice for corporate environmental and human rights abuse.

We work together with local organizations to do research on environmental and human rights violations. Our clients are victims of human rights and environmental rights violations, amongst them indigenous and afro-communities. Our aim is to obtain justice and respect for their land, water and historic rights.

Aguas De Cartagena
in Colombia.

We represent the afro indigenous fisher community in La Rocha Colombia, against Aguas de Cartagena part of the multination of Aguas de Barcelona for polluting their fishing waters.

Agua Zarca Dam Project
in Honduras.

We represent the Lenca Organisation COPINH and the family of the murdered indigenous leader Berta Cáceres against FMO the Dutch development bank. To hold FMO responsible for a breach of their duty to care.

More info ...

Océanos/Manuelita
in Colombia

We represents members of several communities around Isla del Covado, and is seeking to hold the perpetrators of violence, illegal displacement, and environmental damage accountable.
