"We hold multinationals accountable in the western world for environmental and human rights abuses overseas"

We represent our clients in court and work together with local organisations to do research on environmental and human rights violations, to help communities with violations of free prioir and informed consent. We are attorneys that help our clients, indigenous and afro communities, to get justice for their land, water and historic rights.
